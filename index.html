ABOUT ME

I’m an senior at IIT Delhi, pursuing electrical engineering as my major degree. I'm passionate about contributing to the global R&D effort to unearth more about the unknown about ourselves, the world around us, and inculcate sustainability into our lives - with the lessons I've learnt and the subsequent tools I have shaped for myself while working in the intersection between the domains of intelligent systems, control and signal processing. I am also extremely curious to know more deeply about how people think, respond to various stimuli and interact with living and non-living components of his/her environment - and using this knowledge to design unique end-solutions to intimately cater to needs and aspirations of a given target-group of people. It is in an attempt to answer to this curiosity that I currently pursue studying various facets of user-centered design, and human-computer interaction in parallel to my major.

Oh also, I am also really into music - I love a good beat as much as I love slow ballads and R&B. Just FYI :)
