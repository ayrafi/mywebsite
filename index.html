ABOUT ME

I’m an senior at IIT Delhi, pursuing electrical engineering as my major degree with a minor in user-centered design. I'm passionate about contributing to the global R&D effort to unearth more about the unknown about ourselves, how we interact to the world around us, and inculcate sustainability into various points in the design process of products/services/PSS-es.

I believe, in the current landscape, with AI, IoT becoming increasingly ubiquitous and pervasive, design offers a rare and feasible shot at manually injecting atleast a fraction of that feeling of organic consciousness into this rapidly exploding landscape. Moving ahead, designing computation-powered and tech-powered experiences should progress towards incoporating a more natural mode of interaction with humans and empowering the user by encouraging self awareness. This is vital, in order for users to develop a sense of trust and transparency and avoid destroying the user's mental well-being.

With the lessons I've learnt (and continue to learn), the experience gained, and the subsequent tools I have shaped for myself while working in the intersection between EE, CS, design and the human mind- it is my aspiration to create tech-powered experiences caterting to various speheres of people's existences that will enrich their lives, and intimately equip them with all the tools needed to tackle the world while honoring each individual's unique perspective and style.

Oh also, I am also really into music - I love to bob around to good beat as much as I love moving around to slow ballads and R&B. Just FYI :)
