TASK TWO

PROJECT OUTLINE

The first few weeks of classes flew by with discussions and brain storming sessions on deciding a topic for our project. My team consists of Aryan Choudhary, Gauransh Soni (both sophies) and Navaneeth KP (a junior). An overview over what we plan to do in the next few weeks is outlined here.

We decided to tackle the problem of supply chain optimization for the much awaited COVID-19 vaccine. We aim to develop a software that will be able to predict, given the limited number of vaccines that will be availabe in the initial stages - how can we decide to whom all should we give the vaccination first as to ensure minimization of cases in the future? We plan to implement this using a prediction software for predicting the trend of how COVID-19 will spread in the future, and trying to comprehend the impacts of this model on vaccinating a certain number of people in the region.
